About — Mission & Vision

Bano Qabil exists to empower youth through industry-aligned skills training, real projects, and employer connections. We focus on practical outcomes: portfolios, freelancing, and job placement.

Mission:

Deliver accessible, high-quality digital skills training that leads to income generation and employment.

Vision:

Become a leading regional skills academy known for placing graduates in the digital economy.

Values:

  • Practical learning
  • Integrity
  • Inclusion
  • Industry partnership

Policies

  • Attendance: 80% minimum attendance required for certificate eligibility.
  • Refund: Every Student will be pay 3000 before the starting of course. After completion the course institute will return the 3000 fund
  • Code of Conduct: Respectful behaviour, no harassment, copyright compliance.
  • Data Privacy: Student data stored securely; shared with partners only with consent.

How scholarships work

Documents required (if applying for need-based): ID, proof of income (family), recent photograph.

Scholarships & Sponsorships — Detailed

Merit Scholarship

Who: Top applicants based on practical task & interview.

Benefit: Up to 100% tuition waiver, mentorship sessions, priority placement.

Apply: Select "Merit" in the form and submit a sample task (link to GitHub or portfolio).

Selection: Shortlisting → practical task → interview → final offer.

Need-Based Sponsorship

Who: Learners from low-income households with supporting documents.

Benefit: Partial fee coverage, monthly transport stipend (if available).

Apply: Select "Need-based" and upload supporting documents at the center or send to scholarships@banoqabil.org

Women-in-Tech Grants

Who: Female applicants keen to pursue tech/design careers.

Benefit: Fee discounts, mentorship, & networking opportunities.

Apply: Select "Women-in-Tech" and provide a short statement (250 words) explaining your goals.

Web Development — 16-week Syllabus & Projects

  1. HTML fundamentals, semantic tags, accessibility.
  2. CSS basics, layout (Flexbox, Grid), responsive design.
  3. JavaScript fundamentals, DOM manipulation.
  4. AJAX & fetching APIs, JSON.
  5. Intro to backend (Node.js & Express), simple REST API.
  6. Authentication basics, forms, validation.
  7. Deployment (Netlify/Heroku), Git & GitHub workflow.
  8. Final capstone: Personal portfolio & small e-commerce demo.

Capstone deliverable: Live portfolio site with 3 projects and a blog post.

E-Commerce — 16-week Syllabus

  1. Store selection: Shopify vs WooCommerce, setup basics.
  2. Product catalog & photography.
  3. Payment gateway integration & testing.
  4. Shipping & return workflows.
  5. Simple digital marketing for stores (ads & SEO).
  6. Analytics & conversion improvements.
  7. Store launch & testing.
  8. Project: Live mini-store with 10 products.

Digital Marketing — 16-week Syllabus

  1. Introduction to digital marketing & buyer journeys.
  2. SEO basics & keyword research.
  3. Content strategy & calendars.
  4. Paid advertising fundamentals (Facebook, Google).
  5. Analytics (Google Analytics) & KPIs.
  6. Conversion rate optimization basics.
  7. Campaign planning & execution.
  8. Project: Run a small paid campaign for a local business.

Graphic Designing — 16 week Syllabus

  1. Design principles & color theory.
  2. Adobe Photoshop basics.
  3. Adobe Illustrator basics & vector work.
  4. Branding & logo design process.
  5. Print layout & production basics.
  6. Project: Brand identity pack & portfolio.
